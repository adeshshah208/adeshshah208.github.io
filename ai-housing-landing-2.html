How these numbers are worked out
Figures are indicative, on standard 2026 Maharashtra rates:
  • Stamp duty (incl. cess): Mumbai, Navi Mumbai & Vasai-Virar 6%; Thane & Pune 7% — minus 1% when registered in a woman's sole name.
  • Registration: 1%, capped at ₹30,000.
  • GST: nil on ready-to-move (with OC); 5% on under-construction, 1% if it's affordable housing (up to ₹45L).
  • EMI uses the standard reducing-balance formula on the loan amount.
Your real cost depends on the ready-reckoner rate, carpet area, builder charges and loan terms — none of which a brochure spells out. Pressure-testing those for your deal is what the consultation is for.
