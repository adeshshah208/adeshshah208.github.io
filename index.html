How these numbers are worked out
These figures are indicative, based on standard 2026 Maharashtra rates.
  • Stamp duty (including cess): 6% in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Vasai-Virar, 7% in Thane and Pune, and 1% lower when the home is registered in a woman's sole name.
  • Registration: 1% of the price, capped at ₹30,000.
  • GST: nothing on ready-to-move homes with an OC, 5% on under-construction, or 1% if it qualifies as affordable housing under ₹45 lakh.
  • EMI uses the standard reducing-balance formula on the loan amount.
Your real cost depends on the ready-reckoner rate, carpet area, builder charges and loan terms, none of which a brochure spells out. Going through those for your own deal is what the consultation is for.
